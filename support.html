Frequently asked questions

About the app

What is Owe Book?
Owe Book is a personal lend-and-borrow tracker — a private notebook for keeping track of who owes what between you and the people in your life. Record a lend or borrow, add a note or receipt, and Owe Book keeps a running balance for each person and lets you settle up. It's a record-keeping tool, not a payment app: it never moves money.

Is the app free?
Yes, Owe Book is free to download and use. An optional one-time purchase, Owe Book Pro, unlocks extras like multiple currencies, iCloud sync, group bill-splitting, and statement export — no subscription.

Purchases

How do I unlock Owe Book Pro?
Open Settings → Unlock Owe Book Pro (or tap any Pro feature) and confirm the one-time purchase through Apple.

How do I restore a purchase?
On a new device or after reinstalling, open the paywall and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed into the App Store with the Apple ID you used to buy Pro. Apple is the source of truth for what you own.

I was charged but Pro is still locked.
Tap Restore Purchases first — that resolves almost all cases. If it's still locked, email us and we'll help.

Can I get a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple, not by us. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in, find the Owe Book purchase, and request a refund.

Using the app

How do I add a person?
Tap to create a new entry and choose a person — type a name and number, or pick someone from your Contacts. Only the contact you select is used; your address book is never uploaded.

How do I record a lend or borrow, and settle up?
Create an entry, choose lend or borrow, and enter the amount, date, and an optional note or receipt image. When it's paid back, open the entry and mark it settled (fully or partially).

How do I split a bill?
With Owe Book Pro, create a group, add members, and add a shared expense — Owe Book splits it and tracks who owes what within the group.

How do I export a statement?
With Owe Book Pro, open a person or group and use the export/share option to generate a statement you can share wherever you like.

How do I sync across my devices?
Turn on Settings → iCloud Sync (Owe Book Pro). Your ledger syncs through your own private iCloud account, and stays consistent across your Apple devices.

How do I lock the app?
Turn on Settings → Require Face ID. Owe Book will ask for Face ID or your device passcode each time you open it.

Privacy and data

What data does the app collect?
Your ledger stays on your device (and your own private iCloud if you enable sync) — we never see it. We collect only anonymous usage analytics and crash reports through Firebase, never linked to your identity and never including your ledger content. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.
